Romania

By the early 2000s, The Grand Royal Hotel had regained its status as a five-star landmark, attracting international tourists, diplomats, and business travelers. Its award-winning restaurant specializes in traditional Romanian cuisine, while its elegant ballroom has once again become a sought-after venue for weddings and high-profile events. Today, The Grand Royal Hotel remains one of Romania’s most prestigious hotels, blending history with contemporary comfort. As a symbol of resilience and elegance, it continues to tell the story of Romania’s evolving identity—one guest at a time.
